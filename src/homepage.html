About Us!

At “Capture the Adventure”, we don’t just go on vacations, we create memorable adventures for years to come and also share our explorations with a like-minded community. “Capture the Adventure” has been around for over 50 years, and we started as a small group of hikers who loved to capture amazing wildlife and landscape photos while camping or backpacking off-the-beaten paths. Our unique adventures as a group of friends inspired us to create a community of like-minded individuals who also enjoyed nature through the lens and wanted to create a larger experience for beginners to get started in the outdoor world.



JOIN OUR ENTHUSIASTS TODAY



If you found us through our social media, you will know that we offer a unique journey to the outdoors through amazing wildlife and landscape photography. We stand apart from other tour companies in that we offer unique planning and expert guidelines to help you become an expert of your own. We spend more time in the places we visit to try and get the one-of-a-kind shots of the wildlife and people we encounter on the way. Our trips are specifically designed for not only interested photographers, but outdoor and adventure enthusiasts alike. We offer exclusive experiences in the field to work on your photography skills as well as your hiking, camping, survival, and backpacking skills to help you become a well-rounded adventure photographer. Additionally, we hope to connect you with other like-minded individuals to help grow this community of future explorers.
